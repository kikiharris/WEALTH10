Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2025 · Last Updated: May 1, 2025

Overview

Billionaire Society ("we," "us," or "our") operates the credit intelligence platform located at this website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

By using our platform, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

Information We Collect

When you use our platform, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Credit report data that you voluntarily upload (processed in real time and not permanently stored on our servers)
  • Personal information you provide in our questionnaire, including gender, veteran status, business name, industry, and financial information
  • Payment information processed securely through Stripe (we never see or store your card details)
  • Basic usage data such as browser type, device type, and pages visited

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide exclusively to:

  • Generate your personalized credit and funding analysis report
  • Process your payment securely through Stripe
  • Deliver your results and funding recommendations
  • Improve the accuracy and quality of our analysis system

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

Credit Report Data

Your credit report PDF is processed in real time. It is transmitted securely over HTTPS to our AI analysis system (powered by Anthropic) and is not permanently stored on our servers. Once your report has been analyzed and your results generated, the raw report data is not retained.

Your analysis results are temporarily stored in your browser's local storage on your own device. This data is only accessible on the device you used and is cleared when you click "Start Over" or clear your browser data.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our platform:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Stripe is PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant. We never receive or store your credit card information. View Stripe's privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy
  • Anthropic — AI analysis engine. Your credit report text is sent to Anthropic's API for analysis. Anthropic does not use API data to train their models. View their policy at anthropic.com/privacy
  • Vercel — website hosting and serverless functions. View their policy at vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement the following measures:

  • All data transmitted to and from our platform is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • API keys and sensitive credentials are stored as encrypted environment variables and never exposed to users
  • We do not maintain a database of user credit reports or personal financial information
  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe using bank-level encryption

While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.
